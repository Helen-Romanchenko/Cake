About us

About the confectioner

The confectioner as a calling

Pavel is a professional chef in various gastronomic areas, but one day his heart conquered the confectionery art.

From an early age, he was fond of sophisticated craftsmanship and turned to his talents as a confectioner.

Principle - natural ingredients

Today Pavel pleases with products not only loved ones, but also those who prefer high-quality products for celebrations, and the main principle is the choice of natural ingredients.

I'm for simple recipes. In Italy it is called cucina povera: when ordinary ingredients turn into a divine dish.
